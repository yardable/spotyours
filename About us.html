About us

SpotYours is an application that aims to connect people who share the same passions in a single place. After choosing your community (skateboarder, photographer, etc.), you can share and find places that match you the most. You can interact with other peers, give your opinion and even share your opinion and even share photos on the different places. Our goal is to create a link between everyone through common passions.
